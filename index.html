About Me

A short story about who I am, what I enjoy building, and what I'm aiming for next.

Hi, I’m Sinura Damsath Perera — a passionate student, creative designer, and aspiring full-stack web developer from Sri Lanka 🇱🇰. I enjoy building modern websites, exploring new technologies, and creating digital experiences that are both functional and visually appealing.

I have a strong interest in ICT, web development, graphic design, branding, and AI-powered creativity. Over the years, I’ve worked on different school and personal projects including presentations, brand identity designs, websites, and interactive applications. I love turning ideas into reality through clean design and creative problem-solving.

As a developer, I mainly work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. I’m also continuously improving my UI/UX design skills to create professional and user-friendly interfaces. Besides coding, I enjoy creating digital artwork, cinematic edits, and social media content.

I believe in learning something new every day and always pushing myself to improve. My goal is to become a skilled software engineer and create impactful digital products that inspire people around the world. When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll probably find me exploring new tech trends, working on creative ideas, or helping others learn about technology.

About Me

A short story about who I am, what I enjoy building, and what I'm aiming for next.

Hi, I’m Sinura Damsath Perera — a passionate student, creative designer, and aspiring full-stack web developer from Sri Lanka 🇱🇰.

Currently studying at Ananda College, Colombo, I have a strong interest in technology, creativity, and building modern digital experiences that combine clean design with functional development.

I work with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. I enjoy creating responsive websites, interactive user interfaces, and full-stack applications that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

Alongside web development, I also explore UI/UX design, branding, digital artwork, cinematic visual editing, and creative content creation. I love experimenting with modern layouts, typography, animations, and visual aesthetics to create professional and engaging projects.

I’m constantly learning, improving my skills, and challenging myself through real-world projects and creative ideas. From portfolio websites and branding concepts to ICT projects and full-stack applications, I enjoy turning ideas into impactful digital experiences.

My goal is to continue growing as a developer and designer while building innovative projects that combine creativity, technology, and modern design.
